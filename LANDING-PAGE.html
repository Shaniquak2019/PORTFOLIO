LANDING PAGE

DURATION OF PROJECT COMPLETION:
1/1/20 - 1/22/20

This piece was designed as a landing page for artist, Chris Brown. I was inspired to do this page for him because of his strong connection to art. Aside from music, Chris has a deep passion for designing and creating artwork. These interests really show in his official website. I was drawn to his site because everything is very vibrant and "pops". I included similar colors in my piece along with a mural that was handmade by Chris.

***IMPORTANT***

I am only responsible for the landing page on this project ! The nav links were simply added to direct traffic from my LANDING PAGE to the OFFICIAL website.
