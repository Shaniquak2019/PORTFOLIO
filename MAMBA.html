R.I.P. MAMBA

DURATION OF PROJECT COMPLETION: 2/7/20 - 2/14/20

He always gave 2000% in everything he did. He was bigger than basketball. For my story line, I put that Kobe founded this school in 2018; retirement year.

I was always into art so created a dream school that I named in his honor;
MAMBA SCHOOL OF ARTS.
